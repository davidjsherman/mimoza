Knowledge-Based Generalization of Metabolic Models

Download

Model generalization method provides a higher-level view of a metabolic model, by masking inessential details while preserving its essential structure. It groups biochemical species in the model into semantically equivalent classes and generalizes them into their common parent in the ChEBI ontology. The reactions between the same generalized species are factored together into generalized reactions.

Model generalization is implemented as a Python library that takes a model in SBML format as input, and produces two SBML files as output:

  • SBML containing the generalized model,
  • SBML file with groups extension, containing the initial model plus the groups representing species and reaction equivalence classes.

Model generalization method and library were developed by the MAGNOME team at the Inria Bordeaux research center. The library is open source and is available under the CeCILL license (GPL compatible). For any questions and comments please contact anna.zhukova@inria.fr.
